== Description ==

Crunchy Recipe is a free recipe WordPress theme creating an engaging food and recipe website. The theme is designed to work seamlessly with the Delicious Recipes plugin, providing numerous features that simplify the process of creating and sharing your culinary creations with your audience. This mobile-friendly theme is SEO optimized to enhance your recipe visibility on search engines. Crunchy Recipe is optimized for speed, ensuring your site loads quickly and ranks higher on search engines. The theme offers unlimited colors and over 1400 Google fonts, allowing you to modify your website's aesthetics with a single click. The pre-defined Instagram section makes it easy to display your food photographs and grow your followers. It also incorporates newsletter integration to assist you in expanding your email list and cultivating strong relationships with your readers. Crunchy Recipe is translation-ready, RTL-ready, and fully compatible with WooCommerce. Check the demo at https://wpdelicious.com/wordpress-themes/crunchy-recipe/, and get support at https://wpdelicious.com/support-ticket/.

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload Theme and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.
